RV VR is a game about taking your family for tacos in a Eurobeat-backed cosmos. It was made in 48 hours for Global Game Jam 2019, and it won honorable mention at GGJ at GSU. I made it with WinaGodwin Anwanyu, Sean Fisher, Alice Kalok, and Kyle Perras. I designed the UX/UI and contributed the UI art.

play game
github repo

Dao Duck Goose is a wuxia waterfowl fighting game I developed for my final project in a class at Georgia Tech. It's about a duck with a sword fighting a lot of other ducks with swords. I created all art, and I coded it entirely in C. The music is by me and Kevin MacLeod (incompetech.com). The ROM is playable using any GBA emulators or with a flash cartridge on a physical GBA.
